About Us: Unleashed Pawsapalooza!

Welcome to Unleashed Pawsapalooza

Unleashed Pawsapalooza is where fashion-forward Chiweenies rule the runway! We blend modern trends with the vibrant spirit of traditional Filipino clothing to redefine luxury dog fashion. Our brand is a celebration of style, comfort, and the undeniable cuteness of our four-legged friends.

Inspired by the rich heritage of the Philippines, our designs pay homage to the elegance and beauty of traditional Filipino clothing. From intricate patterns to vibrant hues, we infuse these elements into our collections, creating a unique blend of cultural appreciation and contemporary fashion.

Founder C. Arboleda, a proud Filipino and devoted dog lover, was inspired to create luxurious and stylish garments specifically designed for long dogs like her Chiweenie companion. Frustrated with the lack of options that catered to long-bodied dogs, she embarked on a mission to change the game.

At Unleashed Pawsapalooza, we believe that fashion should never sacrifice comfort. Our carefully tailored garments ensure a perfect fit, allowing your Chiweenie to strut their stuff with ease. Each piece is a labor of love, handcrafted by skilled artisans using only the finest materials.

Join the Unleashed Pawsapalooza family and embrace fashion-forwardness, individuality, and a good dose of humor. Let your dog's inner fashionista shine and say, "Bow-WOW!" Together, let's make a statement, one paw at a time.
